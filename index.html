Chrome Extention

Our chrome extention is the easiest way to find fake news throughout your social media and news feed.

magic highlighting

As you scroll through your social media or other news feeds, the extention highlights fake articles like magic.

flags content

Beyond that, the extention flags malicious or misleading content so you know which sources to stay away from.
